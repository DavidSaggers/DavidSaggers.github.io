Welcome to my Portfolio Website!

I have been interested in 3D design and digital art since I started using computers. I studied Art & Design and Media in college in the hopes it could improve my artistic and digital skills.

I have been using software like photoshop and premier pro for a few years now, but more recently I started creating 3d models with blender and I am very driven towards learning new skills with 3d modelling and making good looking models.

Here is some of my work, a portfolio with images of what I have created, and a video which includes an animation of one of my models and more detail into how these models and environments were created.
