Project - Leviathan Axe
This was my attempt at creating the leviathan axe from God of War. while creating this I learned how to UV unwrap and texture objects, how to use modifiers such as multi-resolution, subdivision surface and shrink wrap and how to make a normal maps from a high poly mesh to be applied to a lower poly mesh.

Project - City Street
I created this city street and wanted to try out lighting with the cycles rendering engine in blender which has ray tracing, I experimented with different settings for rendering and learned about render samples and denoising. The renders with this engine turned out very noisy or blurry with a denoiser active. The simpler Eevee render engine did not have any noise however lacked lighting details.
