Leviathan Axe
This was my attempt at creating the leviathan axe from God of War. while creating this I learned how to UV unwrap and texture objects, how to use modifiers such as multi-resolution, subdivision surface and shrink wrap and how to make a normal maps from a high poly mesh to be applied to a lower poly mesh.

Mechanical Hammerhead Shark
I created this Mechanical Hammerhead Shark as I am very interested in both ocean life and the mechanical/steampunk style so I wanted to combine to two to make something unique. with this model I learned how to rig a model and animate it, how to create a glass effect and how to use nodes to create an ocean enviroment.

City Street
I created this city street and wanted to experiment with raytraced lighting using the cycles rendering engine used in blender, I experimented with different settings for rendering and learned about render samples and denoising. The renders with this engine turned out very noisy or blurry with a denoiser active. The simpler Eevee render engine did not have any noise however lacked lighting details.
